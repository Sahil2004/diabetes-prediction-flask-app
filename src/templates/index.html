About This Predictor

This application predicts the likelihood of a person developing gestational diabetes based on clinical inputs. Enter the required details in the form above and click "Predict" to get the result.

How to Interpret Results

  • Will not develop gestational diabetes — Indicates a low risk based on the input values.
  • Will develop gestational diabetes — Indicates a high risk and recommends consulting a healthcare provider.

Typical Ranges (non-diagnostic)

  • Glucose below 120 mg/dL is generally considered safe.
  • BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered healthy.
  • Normal blood pressure is typically around 80 mm Hg diastolic.
